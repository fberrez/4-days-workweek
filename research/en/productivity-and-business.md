## 1. Major Trials and Studies

### Microsoft Japan (August 2019)

- **Scale**: 2,300 employees given every Friday off for the full month of August
- **Productivity**: Sales per employee rose **39.9%** compared to August 2018
- **Electricity costs**: Decreased **23.1%**
- **Paper printing**: Reduced by nearly **60%**
- **Employee approval**: **92.1%** of employees said they liked the four-day workweek
- **Meeting changes**: Meetings were capped at 30 minutes with a maximum of 5 attendees
- **Source**: [NPR](https://www.npr.org/2019/11/04/776163853/microsoft-japan-says-4-day-workweek-boosted-workers-productivity-by-40), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2019/11/04/microsoft-japan-4-day-work-week-experiment-sees-productivity-jump-40percent.html), [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2019/11/04/tech/microsoft-japan-workweek-productivity/index.html)

### Iceland Trials (2015-2019)

- **Scale**: 2,500+ workers (over 1% of Iceland's working population), public sector
- **Hours**: Reduced from 40 hours to 35-36 hours per week, no pay reduction
- **Productivity**: Maintained or improved across the majority of workplaces
- **Worker wellbeing**: "Dramatically" improved across stress, burnout, health, and work-life balance
- **Sick days**: Employees took fewer sick days overall
- **Long-term impact**: Icelandic trade unions negotiated reduced hours for tens of thousands of members nationwide. Iceland's productivity growth has since led the Nordic countries over the subsequent five years
- **Source**: [Autonomy Institute Report (June 2021)](https://autonomy.work/portfolio/icelandsww/), [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2024/10/25/business/iceland-shorter-working-week-economy), [WEF](https://www.weforum.org/stories/2022/01/four-day-week-work-life-balance-trial/)

### UK Pilot (June-December 2022)

- **Scale**: 61 companies, ~2,900 workers -- the world's largest 4-day workweek trial
- **Model**: 100:80:100 (100% pay, 80% time, 100% productivity)
- **Revenue**: Increased **1.4%** on average (weighted by company size); **35%** increase compared to equivalent prior-year periods
- **Sick days**: **65%** reduction
- **Burnout**: **71%** of employees reported lower burnout levels
- **Stress**: **39%** of employees reported reduced stress
- **Staff turnover**: Dropped **57%** during the trial
- **Continuation**: **56 of 61** companies (92%) were still operating a 4-day week one year later; **31 companies** (51%) made it permanent
- **Source**: [Autonomy Institute](https://autonomy.work/portfolio/uk4dwpilotresults/), [4 Day Week Global](https://www.4dayweek.com/uk-pilot-results), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2024/02/22/four-day-working-week-most-firms-in-worlds-biggest-trial-stick-to-it.html), [NPR](https://www.npr.org/2024/02/27/1234271434/4-day-workweek-successful-a-year-later-in-uk)

### 4 Day Week Global -- US/Ireland Pilot (2022)

- **Scale**: 33 companies, ~1,000 employees (US, Ireland, Australia)
- **Revenue**: Average revenue rose **38%** compared to same period the previous year; **8.1%** increase across all companies during the trial period
- **Overall experience**: Companies rated the experience **9 out of 10**
- **Continuation**: Almost all companies committed to or planned to continue with the 4-day schedule
- **Source**: [4 Day Week Global](https://www.4dayweek.com/us-ireland-results), [CNN](https://edition.cnn.com/2022/11/30/business/4-day-work-week-results)

### Spain Government Trial (2021-ongoing)

- **Scale**: Government-funded three-year trial launched September 2021, involving 200-400 companies
- **Model**: 100-80-100 (100% pay, 80% time, 100% output)
- **Notable result**: DESOL (tech company) reported a **20% increase in sales** and **20% decrease in absenteeism**
- **Health impact**: The trial led to healthier workers and less pollution
- **Source**: [WEF](https://www.weforum.org/stories/2023/10/surprising-benefits-four-day-week/), [Euro Weekly News](https://euroweeklynews.com/2025/10/31/uk-spain-europe-trial-4-day-working-week-do-we-really-need-it/)

### Germany Trial (2023-2024)

- **Scale**: 45 organizations, ~900 employees across diverse sectors
- **Continuation**: **73%** of participating companies chose to continue with the four-day structure
- **Meetings**: Frequency and duration of meetings reduced by **60%**
- **Sleep**: Participants gained an average of **38 extra minutes of sleep**
- **Retention**: An **8-point increase** in retention rates
- **Source**: [Euro Weekly News](https://euroweeklynews.com/2025/01/12/germanys-four-day-work-week-proves-to-be-a-massive-hit/), [Euronews](https://www.euronews.com/next/2024/02/02/the-four-day-week-which-countries-have-embraced-it-and-how-s-it-going-so-far)

### Portugal Pilot (2023)

- **Scale**: 41 private-sector organizations, 1,000+ employees, 6-month pilot starting June 2023
- **Results**: Workers reported improvements in work-life balance, reduced stress, and lower burnout
- **Continuation**: Many companies considered making the change permanent
- **Source**: [Euronews](https://www.euronews.com/next/2024/02/02/the-four-day-week-which-countries-have-embraced-it-and-how-s-it-going-so-far), [Protime](https://www.protime.eu/en/news/4-day-work-week-eu)

### South Africa Pilot (March-August 2023)

- **Scale**: 28 South African businesses and 1 Botswanan business
- **Company satisfaction**: Rated their experience **8.0 out of 10**
- **Revenue**: **10.5%** average increase in company revenue during the trial
- **Productivity**: **49%** of employees reported increased productivity
- **Burnout**: Dropped for **57%** of participants
- **Absenteeism**: **9%** weighted decrease in sick and personal days
- **Continuation**: **92%** of companies intended to continue or were considering it
- **Employee loyalty**: **51%** said they would need a 21-50% pay increase to go back to five days; **13%** said no amount of money would make them return
- **Source**: [4 Day Week Global](https://www.4dayweek.com/sa-2023-pilot-results), [The Progress Playbook](https://theprogressplaybook.com/2023/11/30/the-surprising-results-of-south-africas-four-day-work-week-trial/)

- **Deep work increase**: Companies like Elephant Ventures found that reserving the first 2 hours for deep work bred more productivity throughout the day
- **Efficiency gains**: At Atom Bank, **92%** of employees changed the way they worked to complete tasks in a shorter timeframe

## 4. Cost Savings

### Direct Operational Savings

| Category | Savings | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Electricity / Energy | **-23%** (one fewer day of operations) | Microsoft Japan, 2019 |
| Paper / Printing | **-59%** | Microsoft Japan, 2019 |
| Variable overhead | **-20%** (one-fifth reduction) | Multiple studies |
| Office cleaning | **~GBP 9,600/year** (for a company paying GBP 4,000/month) | Emburse analysis |
| Office supplies | **~USD 4,000/year** (for a 100-person company) | Emburse analysis |
| Total facilities savings | **~USD 15,200/year** (electricity + supplies + cleaning) | Emburse analysis |

### Employee-Related Cost Savings

- **Reduced turnover costs**: With turnover dropping 57% (UK trial), companies save significantly on recruitment, onboarding, and training. The average cost of replacing an employee is estimated at 50-200% of their annual salary
- **Lower absenteeism costs**: 65% fewer sick days translates directly to reduced lost-productivity costs
- **Commute/transportation**: Companies save on parking subsidies and transit benefits (20% reduction)
- **Workplace perks**: 20% reduction in daily workplace perks spending (meals, snacks, etc.)

### National-Scale Projections

- **UK**: Businesses could collectively save an estimated **GBP 104 billion annually** (Henley Business School)
- **Per-employee savings**: Hybrid/reduced-hour models can save up to **USD 11,000 per employee** (Global Workplace Analytics)

## 7. Company Case Studies

### Perpetual Guardian -- New Zealand (2018)

- **Company**: Estate planning firm, ~200 employees
- **Trial**: 8-week pilot, then made permanent
- **Key results**:
  - Productivity: **+20%**
  - Work-life balance: Improved from **54%** to **78%** of employees
  - Stress levels: **-27%**
  - Team engagement: **+20%**
  - Customer engagement: **+30%**
  - Revenue: Stable, costs decreased
- **CEO Andrew Barnes**: "They went beyond my wildest dreams." Barnes went on to co-found 4 Day Week Global and authored "The 4 Day Week" book
- **Source**: [CNN](https://money.cnn.com/2018/07/19/news/economy/new-zealand-four-day-work-week-perpetual-guardian/index.html), [WEF](https://www.weforum.org/stories/2018/07/four-day-work-week-productivity-new-zealand-trial/)

### Buffer -- Remote/US (2020-present)

- **Company**: Social media management platform, remote-first
- **Key results (3+ years of data)**:
  - **91%** of team members report being happier and more productive
  - **100%** of surveyed employees want to continue for the rest of their careers
  - **73.1%** feel more energized
  - **99.1%** are less stressed
  - **61.5%** find it easier to balance work and life
- **Challenges**: 40.4% feel they cannot finish all work in 4 days; 61.6% use the fifth day as overflow "most or some of the time"
- **Source**: [Buffer Blog](https://buffer.com/resources/four-day-work-week-update/)

### Kickstarter -- US (2021-present)

- **Company**: Crowdfunding platform, ~90 employees
- **Key results**:
  - Employee engagement up **50%**
  - Employees see themselves at Kickstarter in 2 years at **nearly 2x** the previous rate
  - Very low voluntary departure rate since implementation
- **Challenges**: Some teams took longer to adapt, particularly understaffed ones. Holiday weeks (3-day workweeks) did not work; they shifted to working Fridays when Monday is a holiday
- **Source**: [WorkFour](https://workfour.org/case-studies/blog-post-title-four-5l997), [4 Day Week Global](https://www.4dayweek.com/news-posts/case-study-kickstarter-6lawp)

### Bolt -- US (2021-2023)

- **Company**: Financial technology / payments, formerly valued at $14B
- **Pilot results (2021)**:
  - **94%** of workers and **91%** of managers wanted it to continue
  - **84%** of employees said they were more productive
  - **86%** said they were more efficient with their time
  - Job applications increased **200%**
- **Made permanent**: January 2022
- **Later reversed**: Bolt quietly ended the 4-day workweek after CEO Ryan Breslow returned and dismantled several progressive HR policies during a period of financial restructuring and layoffs
- **Lesson**: The 4-day workweek alone cannot sustain a company through fundamental business challenges; however, the pilot results themselves were strongly positive
- **Source**: [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2022/01/05/the-4-day-workweek-becomes-permanent-for-tech-company-bolt.html), [Fast Company](https://www.fastcompany.com/91370026/bolts-ceo-on-why-hes-axing-progressive-workplace-policies)

### Unilever New Zealand (2020-2022, then expanded)

- **Company**: Global consumer goods multinational, 80 NZ employees in pilot
- **Trial**: 18-month pilot (December 2020 - June 2022)
- **Key results**:
  - **Absenteeism**: Dropped **34%**
  - **Stress**: Dropped **33%**
  - **Work-life balance**: **67%** reported improvement
  - **Strength and vigor at work**: Increased **15%**
  - **Stakeholder satisfaction**: **100%** of stakeholders agreed work was completed on time and to high quality
  - Revenue growth remained on track
- **Expansion**: Extended to Australia following positive results
- **Source**: [Unilever](https://www.unilever.com.au/news/press-releases/2022/unilever-australia-new-zealand-expands-fourday-work-week-trial-following-encouraging-results/), [4 Day Week Global](https://www.4dayweek.com/news-posts/unilever-australia-amp-new-zealand-expands-four-day-work-week-trial-following-encouraging-results-srlrx)

### Atom Bank -- UK (2021-present)

- **Company**: Digital-only bank, ~480 employees
- **Model**: 34 hours over 4 days (down from 37.5 over 5), no pay reduction
- **Key results**:
  - **91%** of employees complete all work in 4 days
  - **92%** changed the way they worked to be more efficient
  - Job applications up **49%** (Jan 2022 vs. Jan 2021), and up **33%** after one year
  - Attrition rate decreased by over **22%**
  - Improved sickness absence rate
- **Made permanent**: After successful trial
- **Source**: [Personnel Today](https://www.personneltoday.com/hr/atom-bank-four-day-week-results/), [Atom Bank Blog](https://www.atombank.co.uk/blog/four-day-week-10-months-on/)

### Elephant Ventures -- US (2020-present)

- **Company**: Software and data engineering firm, 41 employees, headquartered in NYC
- **Model**: 10-hour days, Monday through Thursday (compressed schedule)
- **Key results**:
  - Productivity improved **20%** overall
  - Philippines-based team (half the company) saw **20-30%** productivity boost
  - Employees reported better collaboration, reduced stress, improved focus
- **Made permanent**: After a 2-month pilot in August 2020
- **Source**: [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2021/02/25/success/four-day-work-week), [Elephant Ventures Blog](https://www.elephantventures.com/4-day-work-week-pilot/)

### Awin -- Global (2021-present)

- **Company**: Global affiliate marketing network, 1,300+ employees in 17 offices worldwide
- **Model**: Flexible -- either one full day off per week or two half-days off; full salary maintained
- **Key results**:
  - Gross profit grew **13%** annually on average (2019-2022)
  - NPS (Net Promoter Score) improved by **20.7 points**
  - Regrettable turnover decreased **33%**
  - Sick leave days decreased **21%**
  - Staff engagement improved continuously
- **Made permanent**: After 18-month trial
- **Source**: [Awin](https://www.awin.com/us/news-and-events/awin-news/awin-flexible-work-week), [ERE](https://www.ere.net/articles/case-study-awins-four-day-work-week-wonder)

### Exos -- US (2023-2024)

- **Company**: Human performance coaching company
- **Key results (Wharton-analyzed)**:
  - Sales pipeline grew **211%** YoY
  - Turnover fell from **47%** to **29%**
  - Burnout: Dropped from **70%** to **36%**
  - Effective time use: Rose from **67%** to **91%**
  - Manager reviews reflected the same performance level as before
- **Source**: [Fast Company](https://www.fastcompany.com/91072238/wharton-experts-4-day-workweek-pilot-results-exos), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2024/04/05/exos-4-day-workweek-how-it-went-one-year-later.html)

### Wildbit (Postmark) -- US (2017-present)

- **Company**: Software company (maker of Postmark email service)
- **Key results**:
  - Customer support median first reply time: **4.7 hours** (virtually unchanged from pre-transition 4 hours)
  - Customer satisfaction (CSAT): **96%** (up from 95% before)
  - Multi-million dollar products maintained with focused, deep-work approach
- **Philosophy**: Focus on 4 hours of meaningful, cognitively focused work per day rather than volume
- **Source**: [Fast Company](https://www.fastcompany.com/90277702/this-company-shifted-to-a-four-day-workweek-and-its-going-great), [Wildbit Blog](https://www.wildbit.com/4-day-workweek.html)

## 8. Environmental Benefits

### Commute and Transportation

- Commuting time fell from **3.5 hours to 2.6 hours per week** -- a **27%** reduction (4 Day Week Global pilots)
- The UK's carbon footprint could be reduced by **127 million tonnes** per year through shorter working weeks -- equivalent to removing **27 million cars** from the road
- Studies by Boston College and 4 Day Week Global found shorter workweeks are linked to increased cycling and walking

### Energy Consumption

- Microsoft Japan: **23%** decrease in electricity costs
- One fewer day of operations eliminates approximately **20%** of variable energy consumption

### Broader Environmental Research

- A 2012 study (29 OECD nations, data from 1970-2007) found that a **10% reduction** in work hours leads to:
  - **12.1%** decline in ecological footprint
  - **14.6%** decline in carbon footprint
  - **4.2%** decline in CO2 emissions
- **Source**: [Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/climate-solutions/2022/08/08/4-day-workweek-environment/), [Euronews](https://www.euronews.com/green/2025/01/29/work-less-save-co2-how-a-four-day-working-week-can-benefit-the-planet)
